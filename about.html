My Story

Hi, I'm Manuele Frau. I'm a UI/UX designer with a background in art, sound design and customer care. Thanks to my creative mindset and natural tendency to help others I create user-centric design solutions. I have been dealing with customer needs in the hotel and food industry for many years, I have learned to listen and solve their problems by meeting their expectations.

As a UI designer I aim to transform people's needs into products that are simple, aesthetically pleasing, and pixel perfect. I am currently looking for career opportunities for entry-level UX/UI Designer in Italy, Finland and Germany. Remote work is preferred, but I am open to relocating based on job offers within the European Union as well.
